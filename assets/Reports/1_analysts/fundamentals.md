# 📊 Comprehensive Fundamental Analysis Report: SPY (SPDR S&P 500 ETF Trust)

## 1. Overview & Profile

**SPY** is the SPDR S&P 500 ETF Trust, managed by **State Street Global Advisors**. It is the oldest and most heavily traded ETF in the world, designed to track the performance of the **S&P 500 Index** — the benchmark of 500 of the largest publicly traded U.S. companies. As an ETF, SPY does not produce traditional corporate financial statements (balance sheet, income statement, cash flow statement).

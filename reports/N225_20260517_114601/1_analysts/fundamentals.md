## 1. Instrument Identification

**N225** is the ticker symbol for the **Nikkei 225 (Nikkei Stock Average)** — a price-weighted stock market index that tracks 225 top-tier companies listed on the **Tokyo Stock Exchange (TSE)**. It is Japan's oldest and most widely followed stock index, analogous to the Dow Jones Industrial Average in the US.

**Key Facts:**
- **Index Type:** Price-weighted (not market-cap weighted)
- **Exchange:** Tokyo Stock Exchange (TSE)
- **Components:** 225 leading Japanese companies
- **Currency:** Japanese Yen (JPY)
- **Launched:** 1950 (original base: 1949)
- **Owner:** Nikkei Inc.

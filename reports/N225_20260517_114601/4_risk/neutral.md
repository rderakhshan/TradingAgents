This approach acknowledges the early warning signals without overreacting, preserves the long-term bullish thesis while respecting the short-term risk, and avoids the all-or-nothing trap that both the aggressive sell and the full hold present. It’s the sustainable way to compound returns over time—taking some chips off the table, but not folding your hand entirely.
